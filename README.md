# Mark It (Fridge Combo #41)

**Mark It** is a minimalist "One-tap Watermark Inserter" built for creators, photographers, and artists who need to protect their work instantly. Forget heavy editing software—just drop your image and your signature is carved.

---

### **The "Unbeatable Visibility" Logic**
A watermark is useless if it's invisible. **Mark It** uses a "Universal Visibility" algorithm to ensure your signature stands out on any background:

* **High-Contrast Engine:** Combines pure opaque white text with a deep, soft black shadow. Whether your image is a bright snowy landscape or a dark night scene, the signature remains perfectly legible.
* **Smart Scaling:** The font size automatically adjusts based on your image resolution. Your watermark will never be too tiny on a 4K photo or too huge on a small thumbnail.
* **The "clr" Action:** A dedicated clear button in the input field lets you reset your signature in a single tap, making it easy to switch between different tags or brand names.

---

### **Features**
* **1 Action Workflow:** Drop image → Auto-composite → Download.
* **Persistent Memory:** Your signature is saved in your browser (LocalStorage). Next time, just drop the file.
* **Universal Save Guide:** Clear instructions for iOS (Files app) and Android/PC (Downloads folder) users.
* **Privacy-First:** Browser-only processing. Your private images never touch any server.

---

### **How to Use**
1.  Type your signature (e.g., `afrorakda © 2026`).
2.  Drop your image or tap to select.
3.  Check the instant preview.
4.  Hit **[DOWNLOAD]** to save your protected work.
